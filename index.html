Our Services

VALIDATION SERVICES ULTRA CLEAN offers total clean room validation. We have two sets of equipments and professional teams to carry out validation. Following Test are carried out by us.

1) Air velocity measurement and air changes calculation.

2)Filter integrity testing (D.O.P./P.A.O.)

3)Air borne particle count test.

4)Recovery test.
